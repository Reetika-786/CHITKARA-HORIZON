Skillverse: Special Courses for Hostellers

Empowering hostel residents with skills for personal and professional growth, managed by the Happiness Club.

About Skillverse

Skillverse is a unique set of courses curated exclusively for hostel residents, offering opportunities to develop new skills, enhance well-being, and boost confidence. These courses are managed by the Happiness Club, ensuring a balanced combination of learning and fun.

Available Courses

  • Yoga: Improve your physical and mental well-being through regular yoga sessions. Designed for beginners and advanced practitioners alike, these classes help improve flexibility, focus, and relaxation.
  • French Language: Learn basic French vocabulary and conversational skills in a fun and interactive environment, perfect for anyone looking to explore new languages.
  • English Language: Enhance your English proficiency with courses focused on grammar, vocabulary, speaking, and writing, tailored to help both beginners and advanced learners.
  • Money-Making Skills: Learn the basics of personal finance, budgeting, and entrepreneurial skills to help you build wealth and make smart financial decisions.
  • Build Confidence: Develop your confidence and communication skills through engaging workshops aimed at improving public speaking, self-presentation, and leadership abilities.
