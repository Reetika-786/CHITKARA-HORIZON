Hostel and University Sports Activities

Stay active with a variety of indoor and outdoor sports at our university and hostel facilities.

Sportium: The University Sports Complex

The Sportium is the university's state-of-the-art sports complex that offers a range of indoor and outdoor activities for all students. Whether you're into basketball, tennis, football, or swimming, the Sportium has something for everyone.

Outdoor Sports

  • Football and Cricket Fields
  • Tennis and Basketball Courts
  • Running Track
  • Swimming Pool

Indoor Sports

  • Badminton and Squash Courts
  • Table Tennis
  • Gymnasium with modern equipment
  • Yoga and Aerobics Studio

Indoor Games in the Hostel

In addition to the Sportium, our hostel offers a selection of indoor games to enjoy during your leisure time, including:

  • Table Tennis
  • Carrom
  • Chess
