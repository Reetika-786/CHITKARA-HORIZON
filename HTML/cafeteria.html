A barista is a skilled coffee professional who specializes in preparing and serving espresso-based drinks, such as lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos. Working primarily in coffee shops, a barista is responsible not only for crafting high-quality beverages but also for creating a welcoming environment for customers. Their role often involves grinding coffee beans, steaming milk, and maintaining espresso machines, all while managing a fast-paced work environment. Beyond technical skills, a barista needs to have excellent customer service abilities, as they often engage with customers, recommend drinks, and handle transactions.
Many baristas take pride in the artistry of coffee-making, often learning to create intricate latte art.
