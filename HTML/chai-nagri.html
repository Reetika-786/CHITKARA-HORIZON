"CHAI NAGRI" is a one-stop destination for freshly brewed multiple variants of the best quality traditional Indian Chai and multiple food options.
Behind a great cup of tea is a complex process involving many talented people, years of nurturing, a wealth of technology & continuous innovation. We combine premium quality Assam tea leaves with natural spices from all over the world. We maintain consistency in quality by tasting each batch of tea so you can expect the same, consistently delicious, and refreshing cup of tea each time.
Chai has been a big part of the Indian culture and heritage since the early 1900s. Since then the beverage has grown in popularity across the world. There is no one way to make chai, every household has their own recipe. But at the core, chai is Black Tea with milk, sugar and spices. Learn more about this beverage that is known to warm the heart and heal the soul.
The venture is a passion of two IT engineers turned entrepreneurs who decided to collaborate on their business idea and aim to achieve the dream of making our very own Indian Chai, the most popular beverage in the world.
Divam and Simran founded food and beverage startup Chai Nagri, in the corridors of their university in September 2017. We have a strong presence in multiple states like Karnataka, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, Chandigarh, Haryana & the list is expected to be double in next 2 Years.
