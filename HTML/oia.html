Academic Mentorship from some of the best academic institutions from Canada and Australia is Chitkara University’s initiative to bring the world’s best education experience for Indian students, at their doorstep.

Global Transfers

Under our Academic Mentorship from our International partner institutions, we will expose our students to accomplished international faculty who will co-teach at Chitkara University and share their expertise in delivering an applied education for a particular discipline in which students will be enrolled at Chitkara University. We have replicated similar infrastructure and technology that is a global standard. Our partner schools have shared and specified the standards that are required to deliver an International applied pedagogy so that our students can experience similar standards and infrastructure that are available to them here, or any part of the developed world.
we have incorporated similar learning outcomes that is in sync with that of our international partner’s program at their institute in that country. This will help our students with an option to seamlessly transfer to our international partner school with 100% credits, after two years of study at Chitkara University, and based on eligibility conditions.
