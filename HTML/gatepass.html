Gate Pass Approval System

Process Overview

In order to leave the university premises, a student must follow the gate pass approval system. Below are the detailed steps in the approval process:

  • The student raises a request for the gate pass on uHostel.
  • The warden makes a call to the parents to confirm approval.
  • If the parents approve, the warden gives the final confirmation.
  • Once approved, the security is notified, and the gate pass is issued.

Important Information

Ensure that all information is accurate when submitting your request. The warden will verify the details and communicate with your parents.
