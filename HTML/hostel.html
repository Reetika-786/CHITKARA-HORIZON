Hostel Fee(Boys/Girls)

  • Fees are collected semester-wise. The fee cycle is in December for the even semester (January–June) and June for the odd semester (July–December).
  • The fee shall be paid by online mode only. Please visit uHostel to make your payment.
  • Students are expected to pay the full semester fee. Partial payments or installments will not be entertained.
  • Hostel fee once paid will not be refunded or adjusted, except if the candidate leaves the course during the first semester only.
  • Hostel fee is NON-REFUNDABLE if the student is expelled from the hostel on disciplinary grounds.
  • Hostel charges are subject to revision every year.
