About Our Mess Service

Our mess provides a healthy and balanced meal plan with three meals a day: breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We focus on fresh ingredients and a variety of dishes to keep your meals exciting and nutritious.

(a) Students are advised to take good care of their health and to consume food in the authorized Mess. Facility of Mess is provided in the Hostel. Any complaint regarding the Mess is to be taken up with the Warden ON Duty.
(b) The procedure for Mess Complaints would need to be followed in letter and spirit.
(c) Creating ruckus in the Mess or University premise will not be allowed.
(d) Students are not permitted to take mess food to their rooms.
(e) Students are not permitted to bring and eat out side food / cold drinks (Dhaba / restaurants) in the University campus.
(f) Students are permitted to take items available in the University Food Court as well as the cooked home food to their respective mess only.
(g) Students are permitted to take non-perishable food items to their room only after due checking by the security.
(h) Please Do Not Waste Food.
